Capital Reserve = $21,578 @ 99% confidence
PROBLEM
Investment portfolios lack statistically validated risk quantification — making it impossible to set accurate capital reserves or meet Basel III regulatory requirements.
SOLUTION
Built a three-model VaR framework (Historical Simulation, Variance-Covariance, Monte Carlo) with Kupiec's POF backtesting. All models achieved 100% validation, recommending a $21,578 daily capital reserve at 99% confidence.
